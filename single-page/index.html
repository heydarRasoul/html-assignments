Brush

The right brush makes all the difference. Our carefully selected brushes come in various shapes and sizes, offering excellent control and versatility for every stroke. From fine detail work to bold, expressive lines, these brushes are designed to deliver precision, comfort, and long-lasting performance for artists at any level.

Brush Buying Tips:
  • Higher-end brushes live longer
  • Choose the Right Brush Shape:
    • Round: Ideal for detail, line work, and controlled strokes
    • Flat: Great for bold strokes, filling large areas, and blending.
    • Fan: Best for textures, blending, and natural effects like foliage or clouds.
  • Match the Brush to the Medium:
    • Oil Painting: Use sturdy, stiff brushes (like hog hair).
    • Acrylic Painting: Synthetic brushes that hold shape well and withstand water.
    • Watercolor Painting: Soft, absorbent brushes (like sable or squirrel hair) that hold water and pigment.
